Our Unique solution

The only website that uses Machine learning algorithms to identify fake ICOs. Our solution scours the internet and creates a sentiment profile of each ICO, then based on past patterns of fake ICOs; it assigns a probability to the new ICO. The probability falls under one of the three classes High, Medium or Low.

High: means that it is most likely a scam.

Medium: indicates that there is not enough evidence to tip the scale in any direction, so one should do further research.

Low: is the safest rating as per our solution.

This approach guarantees unbiased and authentic ratings based on the current and past market sentiment of ICOs.
